About Dr. Karnav Patel

Dr. Patel has helped thousands regain their confidence by providing expert care in acne, eczema, psoriasis, and cosmetic dermatology. He believes in holistic care, ensuring every patient receives individualized treatment. Over the past decade, he has also pioneered several advanced laser and non-invasive skin resurfacing techniques, catering to patients seeking both therapeutic and cosmetic skin solutions. His commitment to continuous learning and patient education ensures that every individual leaves with clearer skin and better knowledge of long-term skincare.
